PTO types & allotments

PTO TypeStandardSenior / ManagementNotes
Birthday Leave1 day1 dayUsed on or near your birthday
Vacation Leave3–5 days6–9 daysBased on role and tenure
Sick Leave3–5 days5 daysEmergency / health use
Others5 days5 daysBereavement, family events, etc.

How to request PTO

1
Submit via ClickUp form
Go to the PTO Request Form. Fill in your name, role, PTO type, date, and any notes.
2
Give the right notice
Vacation leave: at least 1 week in advance. Emergency / sick leave: at least 2 hours before your shift, whenever possible.
3
Wait for approval
Your direct supervisor reviews and approves. You will see the status update in ClickUp.
4
Confirm and plan handoff
Once approved, inform your team of any tasks that need coverage during your absence.

PTO rules to know

  • PTO does not carry over to the next calendar year unless explicitly approved by Meldee
  • Birthday leave must be used within the birthday month
  • Uninformed absences without an approved PTO request are a conduct violation
  • PTO balances are tracked in the Google Sheets PTO Report — check yours before requesting
  • Questions about your balance? Contact Meldee directly
